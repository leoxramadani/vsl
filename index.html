About

Ismet Vejseli-born on 11.04.1998 in Kumanovo, is a student of singing at the faculty of music ,, S.Cyril and Methodius '' in Skopje, he works with the great soprano Ana Durlovski.

Achievements

Ismet won many singing competitions in Macedonia and many international competitions in Arandjelovac in Serbia. He was the absolute winner of the singing competition in Macedonia two times, and won the first place in other singing competitions four times. Ismet was a contestant in singing competitons in Serbia twice(2013 and 2016), where he won the first place both times. Ismet won the first place in international competition for vocal counterpoint and twice the second place for harmony 1. In 2016 he participated in a music masterclass in Prizren, where he won the opportunity to sing in Forli, Italy and work with Dolores Delgado (soprano from Spain). Ismet now has a few recitals in Macedonia and continues studies with the world famous soprano Ana Durlovski.
